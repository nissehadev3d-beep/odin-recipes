Raw Meat (Tere Sega / Kurt)

Description

This is a common Ethiopian traditional dish which is even served as a gesture (besides doro wet).

This more detailed image of the raw meat shows that this is a purely Ethiopian made meal. The only noticeable ingredient is the lemon. All the ingredients you see are listed below.

Ingredients

  • Injera - A pure Ethiopian dish which is served with almost any dish.
  • Bread - As a ... to eat with.
  • Mitmita - A chili which is used as an appetizer.
  • Lemon - To kill any bacteria on the meat.

Steps

  1. Source high-quality, fresh beef fillet specifically handled for traditional raw consumption.
  2. Slice the meat into long, manageable strips or bite-sized cubes.
  3. Serve immediately alongside warm injera, fresh bread, mitmita, and senafich (mustard dip).
